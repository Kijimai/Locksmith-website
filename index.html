About Us

The business was started in 1969 by owner Roy and his father James where he was also taught the tools of the trade. It has since been passed down to him where he now also teaches his sons the secrets of the craft. Beginning in a humble garage, now expanded into its own building located in the heart of Main St. Sacramento, it remains a family run business that continues to serve their clients with best quality possible.
